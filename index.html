Faiyaz's DevOps Journey

From career crossroads to mastering modern DevOps tools

My journey into DevOps began with a heartfelt conversation with my best friend, who was concerned about my career growth. She suggested enrolling in the DevOps course from StarAgile. Though I lacked strong coding skills initially, learning about cloud platforms opened up immense possibilities. It empowered me to automate deployments, reduce manual errors, and build robust, scalable applications with the help of AI-powered tools. Today, DevOps has become my career accelerator.
