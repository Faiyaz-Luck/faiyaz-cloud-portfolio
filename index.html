My DevOps Journey

My journey began with a conversation with a close friend, who was deeply concerned about my career path. She introduced me to the world of DevOps through StarAgile, encouraging me despite my non-IT background and fear of coding.

As I started learning about Cloud platforms, I realized how powerful DevOps is. It offers faster deployments, higher software quality, and automates everything — reducing manual errors. With the rise of AI, this automation becomes even smarter, allowing multi-node configuration and deployment in one click!

One of the most memorable parts of this journey is how someone close to me gave me belief and direction when I had none. Even when I doubted my coding skills, she inspired me to see DevOps as a field driven by learning and automation.
